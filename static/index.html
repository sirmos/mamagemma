    MamaGemma

    An offline-capable maternal health assistant for community midwives in Nigeria, powered by Gemma 4.

    Gemma 4 · Google DeepMind

    🎯 Purpose

    Community health workers in Nigeria often operate without reliable internet, advanced equipment, or instant specialist access. MamaGemma provides AI-assisted clinical guidance at the point of care — in English and Pidgin.

    ⚡ Key Features

    • Offline-first — works without internet
    • Danger sign detection & triage
    • Multimodal — accepts patient photos
    • English + Nigerian Pidgin output
    • Local consultation log (privacy-first)
    • WHO & FMOH guideline-aligned

    🏥 Designed for

    • Primary Health Care (PHC) workers
    • Community midwives & TBAs
    • Rural clinic health workers
    • Mobile outreach teams

    ⚠️ Disclaimer

    MamaGemma is a decision-support tool. It does not replace clinical judgment. Always refer to a qualified health provider for diagnosis and treatment. In emergencies, call 112 or the nearest hospital.

    🛠️ Technical Stack

    Model: Gemma 4 (E4B for edge deployment, 27B for cloud)
    Backend: Python / Flask
    Deployment: Ollama (offline) / Google AI Studio API
    Frontend: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS — works on any browser, no app install
    Storage: Local JSON — no data leaves the device
